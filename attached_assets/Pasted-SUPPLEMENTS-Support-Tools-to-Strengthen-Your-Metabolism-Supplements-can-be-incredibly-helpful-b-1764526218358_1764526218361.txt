SUPPLEMENTS — Support Tools to Strengthen Your Metabolism

Supplements can be incredibly helpful — but only when the foundation is in place.
If your sleep, food, stress levels, warmth, and daily rhythm are out of alignment, no supplement can override those deeper metabolic needs.

Think of supplements as support, not a standalone solution.
They can calm stress hormones, help with sleep, balance hormones, improve digestion, increase energy, and support thyroid function — when paired with the right lifestyle choices.

Every woman is unique. Your needs, your metabolism, your stress levels, and your symptoms are individual.
If you’re unsure where to start, it’s always best to talk to someone who understands this approach and can guide you.

Below are the supplements most commonly used in the pro-metabolic and bioenergetic space.

⭐ Progesterone (Bioidentical USP)

Supports calm, sleep, thyroid health, stress resistance, and balances estrogen.

Often used for:

PMS

Anxiety

Sleep issues

Estrogen dominance

Low mood

Luteal phase symptoms

⭐ Vitamin E (Mixed Tocopherols)

Antioxidant, anti-estrogenic, supports progesterone, protects thyroid, reduces inflammation.

Helps with:

PMS

Breast tenderness

Stress recovery

Hormonal symptoms

⭐ Vitamin K2 (MK-4)

Helps calcium go into bones, supports metabolism, works synergistically with vitamin D.

Great for:

Bone strength

Hormone balance

Metabolic healing

⭐ Vitamin D3 (Preferably paired with K2)

Boosts mood, immunity, hormone production, and thyroid function.

Especially helpful for women who work indoors or live in low-sun areas.

⭐ Magnesium (Glycinate, Malate, or Bicarbonate)

Calms the nervous system, supports sleep, lowers cortisol, improves digestion.

Useful for:

Anxiety

Constipation

PMS

Muscle tension

Sleep quality

⭐ Calcium (If dietary intake is low)

Ray Peat emphasized calcium for metabolic stability.

Supports:

Thyroid function

Progesterone

Reducing inflammation

Warmth and energy

Best sources:

Milk

Cheese

Orange juice with calcium

⭐ Gelatin & Collagen

Balances amino acids, supports gut lining, improves sleep, calms nerves.

Easy to add to:

Coffee

Milk

Smoothies

Orange juice

⭐ Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)

Supports energy production, stabilizes blood sugar, lowers lipolysis, reduces stress hormone spikes.

Helps with:

Afternoon crashes

Anxiety

Blood sugar instability

⭐ Taurine

Calms the nervous system, supports bile flow, lowers inflammation.

Useful for:

Bloating

Sleep

Stress response

Gut issues

⭐ Glycine

Calming amino acid that supports sleep, digestion, and nervous system regulation.

Pairs well with collagen or taken alone.

⭐ Aspirin (Low Dose, if tolerated)

Has anti-inflammatory and metabolic-supportive properties in the Peat framework.

May support:

Thyroid function

Stress reduction

Inflammation control

Important: Always start small and monitor.

⭐ Activated B Vitamins (Especially B1 + B2)

Fundamental for energy production, thyroid hormone utilization, and blood sugar stability.

Support:

Mood

Metabolic rate

Cognitive function

Important Reminder

Your metabolism responds to your whole lifestyle, not just a pill.
Supplements work best when paired with:

Regular eating

Nourishing, pro-metabolic foods

Warmth

Good sleep

Lower stress

Gentle movement

Sunlight

If you’re unsure where to begin, talk to someone familiar with the bioenergetic/pro-metabolic approach. Your body’s needs are unique, and the right guidance can make a big difference.
