Your cells have two ways to make energy:

The efficient way (oxidative metabolism): When your cells have enough oxygen and the right nutrients, they burn fuel cleanly and completely. One glucose molecule produces about 32 ATP (energy units), releasing only carbon dioxide and water Ivy Herbal LLC. This is what we want.
The inefficient way (glycolysis/stress metabolism): When your cells are stressed or lacking nutrients, they switch to a backup system that produces only 2 ATP per glucose and creates lactic acid as a waste product.
